"Risa Risque"

Marisa Ashley Quijano is a trained and professional Singer, Actress, Dancer and Producer of the all singing burlesque troupe, the Blacklist Babes Cabaret. Her alter-ego "Risa Risque" is a dark-comedic singing burlesque perfomer, Founder, Producer and Director of the Blacklist Babes Cabaret and Comedy Queen family member, a charity out of the Parliament House Orlando devoted to The Barber FUND, helping those living with cancer.
